## Allocation logic

1. **Damage first:** always invest **2 points into STR** each level.
2. **Stability and feel:** use the 3rd point as a flex point between:
   - **VIT** for survivability
   - **AGI** for smoother speed/skill flow
3. **No LUK/DEF investment** in this 40-level damage-focused window.

## Live adjustment rules

- If you feel too squishy, turn your **next AGI flex point** into **VIT**.
- If damage feels good but movement feels slow, turn your **next VIT flex point** into **AGI**.
- Keep the **2 STR per level** rule unchanged.
