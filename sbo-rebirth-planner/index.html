Stat Reference (click to expand)

Investible Stats — 3 points per level, cap 500 each

STR
+0.4% damage per point (up to +200% at 500). Also adds +10 Stamina at max. Boosts crit damage: crit multiplier scales from ×1 to ×3 based on STR (formula: base × critMulti + base × STRmulti, where STRmulti = STR/500 × 2). Increases multi-hit chance beyond the 50% base.
DEF
Multiplies your gear's Defense stat. Base multiplier is ×5 (5 damage reduction per Defense point). Each DEF point adds +0.01 to the multiplier — at 500 DEF the multiplier is ×10, doubling your flat damage reduction.
AGI
Increases attack speed (faster swings), lowers sword skill cooldowns, and speeds up lower-level skill animations. Also adds +10 Stamina at max and +walk/run speed per point.
VIT
Multiplies your gear's Dexterity stat. Base multiplier is ×10 (10 HP per DEX point). Each VIT point adds +0.01 — at 500 VIT the multiplier is ×15, giving 50% more HP from gear. Also adds +10 Stamina at max and +0.01% resistance to all status effects per point (up to +5% at 500).
LUK
+0.01% crit chance per point (base 15%, up to 20% at 500 LUK). Also increases drop chance from enemies.

Gear Stats — come from equipment, not point investment

Attack
From weapons. 1 Attack = 1 base damage (before STR scaling). Higher weapon skill unlocks higher-Attack weapons.
Defense
From armor and shields. Each point = 5 flat damage reduction (before DEF multiplier). Formula: Defense × (5 + DEF×0.01).
Dexterity (DEX)
From armor and headwear. Each point = +10 max HP (before VIT multiplier). Formula: DEX × (10 + VIT×0.01). Not dodge or accuracy — purely bonus HP.

Other Stats

Stamina
100 + (Level × 5). Maxing STR, AGI, and VIT each add +10 Stamina (total +30 bonus at full investment). Consumed by sprinting, jumping, blocking, and attacking.
Multi-hit
50% base chance to hit twice per swing. Requires 200+ Sword Skill (except 2H, which has it from skill 1). STR and LUK increase the chance beyond 50%. Cap is 65%.
Crit
15% base chance. LUK adds up to +5% (20% max at 500 LUK). Crit damage = (base × critMulti) + (base × STRmulti), where STRmulti = STR/500 × 2. Per-class multipliers: Two-Handed ×1.5 · One-Handed ×2.0 · Rapier ×2.4 · Dagger ×2.7 · Melee ×3.0.
Resistances
Some armor pieces grant 40% resistance to a specific status effect (reduces duration and damage). VIT adds +0.01% resistance to all status effects per point (+5% at 500 VIT).
